Frequently Asked Questions

💰 How much do Fetchers get paid? +

Fetchers earn 75% of the per-minute portion. Example: 30-min Fetch = £7 → Fetcher earns ~£4.50. Tips go 100% to Fetchers.

📏 Is the price based on time or distance? +

Pricing is based on estimated distance converted to minutes. Your price is fixed once requested; traffic does not change it.

📦 What items can Fetch pick up? +

Anything you already own or have paid for: chargers, books, laundry, click & collect orders, prescriptions, keys, documents, groceries, and more.

💳 Do I pay upfront? +

No. Payment is taken after pickup and delivery confirmation.

📍 Can I track my Fetcher? +

Yes, real-time tracking is built into every Fetch.

📊 How does Fetch calculate earnings and costs? +

• Fetcher earnings: 75% of the per-minute portion
• Fetch earnings: 25% of per-minute portion + £1 base fee
• Stripe fee: 1.4% + 20p on total payment

Example – 30-min Fetch (£7):
– Per-minute portion = £6
– Fetcher earns 75% = £4.50
– Fetch earns 25% = £1.50 + £1 base fee = £2.50
– Stripe fee ≈ £0.30
– Fetch net after Stripe = £2.20

🛍️ Can Fetchers buy items for me? +

Not yet. Currently Fetch only collects items you already own. FetchBuy (future) will allow purchases on your behalf.

🛡️ What about insurance and refunds? +

Optional insurance is available. If a Fetch is incomplete, you won’t be charged.
